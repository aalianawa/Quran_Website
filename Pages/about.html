About Us

The Online Quran Academy offers Quran classes worldwide, accessible through any internet-enabled device like a laptop, tablet, or mobile phone. We provide comprehensive courses in Basic Quran Reading, Tajweed, Memorization (Hifz), Tafseer, and Quranic Arabic. With certified instructors from Ejaz ul Quran, our goal is to make learning the Quran easy and accessible from the comfort of your home.

We provide a wide range of courses, including Basic Quran Reading, Quran with Tajweed (pronunciation rules), Quran Memorization (Hifz), Tafseer-e-Quran (explanation of verses), and Quranic Arabic. All our courses are taught by certified and experienced teachers from the Ejaz ul Quran platform. In this digital age, learning the Quran has become more accessible, and we are dedicated to helping students not only memorize the Quran but also understand its deeper meanings and correct recitation. Our mission is to spread this knowledge far and wide, enabling more students to engage in the rewarding task of learning, understanding, and living by the teachings of the Quran.
