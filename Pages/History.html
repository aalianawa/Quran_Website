History

Dr. Yasir Qadhi

An American scholar focused on making the Quran accessible for English-speaking Muslims, with extensive Tafseer series available online. He is the Dean of the Islamic Seminary of America.

Sheikh Ali Gomaa

The former Grand Mufti of Egypt and a prominent scholar, Sheikh Gomaa has written widely on Quranic sciences and provides modern interpretations aligned with Islamic traditions.

Nouman Ali Khan

A Pakistani-American Quran teacher, Nouman is known for his engaging style of teaching the Quran in English. His organization, Bayyinah, offers courses aimed at deepening Quranic understanding among youth.
